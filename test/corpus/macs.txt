---

==================

MACs -hmac-sha2-512-etm@openssh.com

---

(client_config
  (macs
    (macs_value)))

==================
prepend algorithm
==================

MACs ^hmac-sha2-512-etm@openssh.com

---

(client_config
  (macs
    (macs_value)))

==================
use irregular casing
==================

macS hmac-sha2-512-etm@openssh.com

---

(client_config
  (macs
    (macs_value)))

==================
can use equals sign
==================

MACs=hmac-sha2-512-etm@openssh.com

---

(client_config
  (macs
    (macs_value)))

==================
can use equals sign with whitespace
==================

MACs = hmac-sha2-512-etm@openssh.com

---

(client_config
  (macs
    (macs_value)))

==================
can be specified after host
==================

Host example.com
  MACs hmac-sha2-512-etm@openssh.com

---

(client_config
  (host
    (host_value))
  (macs
    (macs_value)))
