==================
specify kex algorithms
==================

GSSAPIKexAlgorithms gss-gex-sha1-,gss-group1-sha1-,gss-curce25519-sha256-

---

(client_config
  (gssapi_kex_algorithms
    (gssapi_kex_algorithms_value)))

GSSAPIKexAlgoRIThms gss-gex-sha1-

  GSSAPIKexAlgorithms gss-gex-sha1-
