==================
use irregular casing
==================

KbdInteractIVEDevices pam

---

(client_config
  (kbd_interactive_devices
    (kbd_interactive_devices_value)))

==================
can use equals sign
==================

KbdInteractIVEDevices=pam

---

(client_config
  (kbd_interactive_devices
    (kbd_interactive_devices_value)))

==================
can use equals sign with whitespace
==================

KbdInteractIVEDevices = pam

---

(client_config
  (kbd_interactive_devices
    (kbd_interactive_devices_value)))
