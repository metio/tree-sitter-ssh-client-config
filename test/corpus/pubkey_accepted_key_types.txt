(client_config

    (pubkey_accepted_key_types_value)))

(client_config

    (pubkey_accepted_key_types_value)))

==================

PubkeyAcceptedKeyTypes -ssh-dss

(client_config
  (pubkey_accepted_key_types
    (pubkey_accepted_key_types_value)))

==================

  (pubkey_accepted_key_types
    (pubkey_accepted_key_types_value)))
